Quick Reader

Quick Reader is a simple and efficient web-based tool designed to help users improve their reading speed and focus. The website provides a clean, distraction-free interface where you can paste any text and read it using rapid word-by-word display techniques. By reducing eye movement and presenting text in a controlled pace, Quick Reader makes it easier to concentrate, understand content faster, and build better reading habits. It’s lightweight, easy to use, and perfect for students, professionals, or anyone looking to boost their reading efficiency online.
